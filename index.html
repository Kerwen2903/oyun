Mint Overview

The Grumbies Eternal Entry is dropping exclusively on OpenSea. This is an access pass that gives you access benefits to all future games in the Grumbies ecosystem. After the mint a snapshot will be announced and holders of the Eternal Entry will be airdropped a Grumbie granting them access to the initial Grumbies Genesis Battle where users can win ETH in an interactive NFT game. This mint is actually for NFTs from 2 collections! The Grumbies Eternal Entry is an access pass to the ecosystem of future Grumbies NFT games, each with their own unique dynamics and gameplay.
